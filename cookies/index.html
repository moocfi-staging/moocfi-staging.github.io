On cookies and privacy

Cookies

A cookie is a small text file that is saved on a users computer. Cookies do not harm the users computer or their data.

This website uses cookies to collect statistical, no-identifying data on its users to improve the website. The data is sent forwards to Google. You can read more on how Google uses the data from here.
